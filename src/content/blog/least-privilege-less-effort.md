Everyone agrees on least privilege in principle. In practice, it collides with
reality: thousands of identities, sprawling permissions, and teams that need to
move fast. The result is over-provisioning that quietly accumulates risk.

This piece looks at how data-driven recommendations and automation make
right-sizing access achievable without grinding productivity to a halt.
