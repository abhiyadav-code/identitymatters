Identity programs have outgrown the in-house, project-by-project model. As
requirements multiply — governance, access reviews, lifecycle, federation —
running it all internally becomes a drag on every other initiative.

Managed identity services offer a different operating model: an expert partner
that runs the identity program as an ongoing service. This piece explores what
that next-generation delivery looks like in practice.
