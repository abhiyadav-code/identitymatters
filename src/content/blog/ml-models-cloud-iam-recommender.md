Behind every "you can safely remove this permission" suggestion is a model
weighing usage history, risk, and uncertainty. Getting those models right is what
makes the difference between recommendations people trust and ones they ignore.

This article explores the machine learning approaches behind Cloud IAM
Recommender — how they learn from access patterns and reason about what's safe to
remove.
