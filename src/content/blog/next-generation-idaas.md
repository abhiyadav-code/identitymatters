The first generation of IDaaS solved single sign-on and basic provisioning. The
next generation is being defined by something larger: adaptive access, rich
governance, and intelligence baked into everyday identity decisions.

This article surveys the capabilities that distinguish modern platforms and what
buyers should look for as the category matures.
