Plenty of vendors will host your identity stack and call it a service. But moving
software to someone else's servers isn't the same as delivering a service. The
real promise of IDaaS is about outcomes — uptime, security posture, and
continuous improvement you don't have to operate yourself.

This article unpacks what the "service" in Identity as a Service should actually
mean, and how to tell genuine managed outcomes from rebranded hosting.
